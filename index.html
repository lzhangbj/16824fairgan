16824 Spring 2022, final project

Non-uniform data distribution is everywhere in the world. Training on such imbalanced datasets will lead to a biased model. In UTKFace dataset, for instance, since white people and young people have become the majority, a trained model could have a biased prior assumption that a person is young and white, without even looking at the image. Accordingly, the accuracy for white and young faces will be much higher. In this project, we aim to train a fair model automatically solving this bias issue in an imbalanced dataset. The source code is available at https://github.com/lzhangbj/16824_fairgan.
