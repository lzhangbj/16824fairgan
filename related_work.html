Solving the Class-Imbalanced Training Data Problem

How to utilize class-imbalanced data to train a fair model has been a popular research problem, which is also known as long-tail image recognition. Existing works can be divided into the following: (1) Design sophisticating loss functions. (2) Resampling of the training data (2) Intra-class distribution transfer from majority class to minority class.
